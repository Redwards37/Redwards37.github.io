Become part of a community focused on empowerment, leadership, and growth.

  4. Get added to our email list and GroupMe for event updates and opportunities.

Membership Requirements

  • Must be a current student at Southern Illinois University Carbondale (SIUC).
  • Have a passion for community engagement, leadership, and personal growth and also possess a high moral character.
  • Commitment to attend regular meetings and participate in at least 2 events per semester.
  • Support the mission and values of BMC, promoting unity and excellence among Black men on campus.
  • Must not have a criminal record without sponsoring chapter approval.
  • Pay $10.00 initiation fee and chapter dues of $20.00 annually.

Executive Board Requirements

  • Be a student in good standing with a minimum of 2.5 GPA.
  • Must have completed two semesters of college.
  • Commit to at least 5 hours of service per month.
  • Attend a mandatory orientation session and/or refresher session.
  • Must not have a criminal record without sponsoring chapter approval.
  • Pay $10.00 initiation fee and chapter dues of $20.00 annually.
  • Devise a plan of service for each academic school year.
