Membership Requirements

  • Must be a current student at Southern Illinois University Carbondale (SIUC).
  • Have a passion for community engagement, leadership, and personal growth and also posses a high moral character.
  • Commitment to attend regular meetings and participate in at least 2 events per semester.
  • Support the mission and values of BMC, promoting unity and excellence among Black men on campus.
  • Member must not have a criminal record without sponsoring chapter approval.
  • Member must pay $10.00 initiation fee and chapter dues of $20.00 annually.

Excutive board requirements

  • Member must be a student in good standing witha minimum of 2.5 GPA.
  • Member must have completed two semesters of college.
  • Member must commit to at least 5 hours of service per month.
  • Member must attend a mandatory oreintation session and/ or refresher session.
  • Member must not have a criminal record without sponsoring chapter approval.
  • Member must pay $10.00 initiation fee and chapter dues of $20.00 annually.
  • Member must devise a plan of service for each academic school year.
